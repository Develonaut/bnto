# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how bnto ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use bnto.io (the "Service"). We built bnto with privacy as a core design principle — your files are processed in your browser and never uploaded to our servers.

---

## The Short Version

- **Your files stay on your device.** All browser tools (compress images, clean CSVs, rename files, convert formats) run entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Files are never uploaded to our servers.
- **No tracking or advertising.** We don't use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party advertising scripts.
- **Minimal data collection.** We collect only what's needed to provide the service — an email address if you create an account, and basic usage data (which tools you use, when, and how long they take).
- **Open source.** Our code is MIT licensed and [publicly available](https://github.com/Develonaut/bnto). You can verify exactly what we do with your data.

---

## What We Collect

### Information You Provide

If you create an account, we collect your **email address** and **name**. You can use bnto without creating an account — all browser tools work without signing in.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Anonymous sessions.** When you use bnto, we create an anonymous session to track your usage within that visit. This does not require any personal information.
- **Usage data.** We record which tools you use, when you use them, and how long processing takes. This helps us understand which tools are popular and identify performance issues.
- **Standard web server logs.** Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard web server information such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This is governed by [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Your files.** Browser tools process files entirely on your device using WebAssembly. Files are never uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on our servers.
- **File contents.** We have no access to the contents of files you process.
- **Third-party analytics.** We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking or advertising service.

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide the service** — manage your account, track usage for quota purposes, and deliver features you've requested.
- **Improve the service** — understand which tools are most used, identify bugs, and improve performance.
- **Communicate with you** — send essential service notifications (such as changes to our terms or policies). We do not send marketing emails.

---

## Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies, all essential for the service to function:

- **Authentication cookies.** Session cookies that keep you signed in. These are HttpOnly and secure — they cannot be read by JavaScript running on the page.
- **Sign-out signal cookie.** A short-lived cookie (expires in 10 seconds) used during the sign-out process to ensure a smooth redirect. It is automatically deleted.
- **Theme preference.** Your light/dark mode preference is stored in your browser's local storage, not a cookie.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party cookies.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate bnto:

- **[Convex](https://www.convex.dev/legal/privacy)** — Database and backend. Stores account data and usage events.
- **[Vercel](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)** — Web hosting. Receives standard web server logs.
- **[Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)** — DNS and CDN. Handles standard web traffic routing.
- **[GitHub](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)** — OAuth sign-in (optional). Receives your email address if you sign in with GitHub.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## Data Retention

- **Account data** is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.
- **Usage data** is retained for analytics and billing purposes. Anonymous usage data may be retained indefinitely in aggregate form.
- **Execution records** (which tool, when, duration) are retained as long as your account is active.

---

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

- Authentication tokens are stored in HttpOnly, secure cookies
- All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS
- Our backend (Convex) provides built-in access controls and data isolation
- Files processed by browser tools never leave your device — the most secure data is data we never have

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
